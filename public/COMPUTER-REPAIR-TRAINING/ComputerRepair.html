Computer Repair Training Services

At OZZO, we offer comprehensive computer repair training designed to create skilled technicians capable of diagnosing and fixing all computer hardware and software issues. Our hands-on courses cover desktops, laptops, and peripherals using professional tools and real-world troubleshooting scenarios.

Types of Computer Repair We Teach

Our computer repair training covers complete hardware and software troubleshooting for desktops, laptops, and computer peripherals. From basic component replacement to advanced motherboard-level repairs, we provide end-to-end technical education.

We train students in motherboard troubleshooting and repair, processor installation and testing, RAM diagnosis, hard drive/SSD replacement, power supply unit repair, cooling system maintenance, BIOS programming, display and graphics card issues, laptop disassembly and reassembly, keyboard and touchpad replacement, and port connectivity problems.

From common issues like slow performance and boot problems to complex motherboard circuit tracing, our curriculum prepares you to handle any computer repair challenge professionally.

Practical Hands-On Workshop

Move beyond theory with our 85% practical training approach. Each student gets individual workstations with real faulty computers, professional toolkits, and live components to practice on. You'll work on actual repair cases under expert guidance until mastery.

Professional Diagnostic Tools & Equipment

We provide training with industry-grade tools including POST test cards, multimeters, soldering stations, rework machines, BIOS programmers, thermal paste applicators, component testers, screwdriver kits, antistatic equipment, and specialized diagnostic software for hardware testing.

Certified Trainers with Real-World Experience

Learn from A+ certified computer repair experts with 10+ years of field experience. Our trainers share not only technical skills but also business insights, client management techniques, and industry best practices for running a successful computer repair service.

Hardware & Software Comprehensive Training

We cover both hardware troubleshooting and software solutions. Hardware training includes component-level repair, circuit analysis, soldering techniques, while software training covers operating system installation, driver troubleshooting, virus removal, data recovery, and network configuration.

Business Setup & Support

OZZO provides complete business guidance for starting your computer repair shop. Our training includes workshop setup planning, tool procurement strategy, spare parts sourcing, service pricing models, marketing approaches, and customer relationship management for sustainable business growth.
