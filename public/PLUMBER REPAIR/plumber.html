Plumber Work Training Services

At OZZO, we offer professional plumbing training programs designed to create skilled plumbers ready for residential and commercial projects. Our hands-on courses cover everything from basic pipe fitting to advanced drainage systems, using industry-standard tools and real-world installation scenarios.

Types of Plumbing Work We Teach

Our comprehensive plumbing training covers all essential aspects of modern plumbing systems for both residential and commercial applications. We prepare students to handle diverse plumbing challenges with confidence and expertise.

We train in water supply line installation and repair, drainage and sewer systems, bathroom and kitchen fitting, CPVC/PVC/copper pipe installation, geyser and water heater connections, sanitary fixture installation, rainwater harvesting systems, gas pipeline fitting, solar water system plumbing, sump and overhead tank installation, and industrial plumbing basics.

From basic household repairs to complex commercial installations, our curriculum ensures you master both traditional techniques and modern plumbing technologies.

Practical Hands-On Workshop Training

We emphasize 85% practical training in our fully-equipped plumbing workshop. Each student works with real pipes, fittings, valves, and tools to practice cutting, threading, joining, and installation techniques under the guidance of experienced plumbing instructors.

Professional Plumbing Tools & Equipment

Students learn to use industry-standard plumbing tools including pipe cutters, threaders, wrenches, soldering kits for copper pipes, PVC glue applicators, pipe bending machines, pressure testing equipment, drain cleaning machines, and modern installation tools for contemporary plumbing systems.

Experienced Plumbing Trainers

Learn from certified master plumbers with 10+ years of field experience. Our trainers share not only technical skills but also practical insights about client handling, site assessment, material estimation, and troubleshooting complex plumbing issues in real-world scenarios.

Modern & Traditional Plumbing Techniques

We cover both conventional plumbing methods and modern innovations. Training includes traditional GI pipe work, contemporary CPVC/PVC installations, concealed plumbing techniques, water conservation systems, and smart plumbing solutions for modern buildings and homes.

Plumbing Business Startup Guidance

OZZO provides complete business support for aspiring plumbing entrepreneurs. Our program includes guidance on tool kit preparation, material sourcing, pricing strategies, contractor registration, client acquisition, quotation preparation, and legal requirements for starting your own plumbing business.
