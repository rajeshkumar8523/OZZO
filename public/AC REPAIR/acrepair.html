AC Repair Training Services

At OZZO, we offer professional AC Repair Training that transforms beginners into skilled HVAC technicians. Our comprehensive program covers installation, maintenance, and repair of all types of air conditioning systems using industry-standard tools and practical, hands-on training methods.

Types of AC Systems We Cover

Our AC repair training program covers all major types of air conditioning systems used in residential, commercial, and industrial settings. We provide comprehensive training on both traditional and modern AC technologies.

We train students in split AC systems (wall-mounted, ceiling, floor-standing), window AC units, central air conditioning systems, cassette ACs, ductable ACs, VRV/VRF systems, portable ACs, and inverter AC technology. Each type receives detailed coverage including installation procedures, troubleshooting methods, and repair techniques specific to that system.

From basic window AC servicing to complex VRV system maintenance, our curriculum prepares you to handle any air conditioning challenge with professional expertise.

Practical Hands-On Training

Move beyond theory with our 85% practical training approach. Each student gets hands-on experience with live AC units, actual tools, and real repair scenarios. You'll practice gas charging, compressor replacement, PCB troubleshooting, and installation procedures under expert supervision.

Professional Tools & Equipment Training

We provide training with professional HVAC tools including digital manifold gauges, vacuum pumps, leak detectors, refrigerant recovery machines, multimeters, clamp meters, anemometers, psychrometers, and specialized AC servicing kits for both split and window units.

Certified Trainers with Field Experience

Learn from licensed HVAC technicians with 10+ years of field experience. Our trainers not only teach technical skills but also share practical insights about customer handling, service call management, pricing strategies, and seasonal maintenance schedules for AC businesses.

Refrigerant Handling & Safety Procedures

We provide complete training in refrigerant types (R22, R32, R410A, R134a), handling procedures, recovery techniques, and safety protocols. You'll learn about environmental regulations, leak detection methods, proper charging techniques, and EPA compliance requirements for AC servicing.

Business Setup & Entrepreneurship Support

OZZO provides complete guidance for starting your own AC repair business. Our training includes shop setup planning, tool procurement advice, spare parts sourcing, service pricing models, marketing strategies for AC services, and customer relationship management for seasonal maintenance contracts.
