Electrician Work Training Services

At OZZO, we provide professional electrician work training designed to create skilled electrical technicians ready for residential, commercial, and industrial projects. Our comprehensive courses combine theoretical knowledge with hands-on practice, ensuring you master safe and efficient electrical installation, maintenance, and repair techniques.

Types of Electrical Work We Teach

Our electrician training program covers a complete range of electrical systems and applications for different environments. Whether you're interested in domestic wiring or industrial electrical projects, we provide comprehensive skill development.

We train students in house wiring (single phase & three phase), switchboard and DB installation, light fitting and fan installation, electrical conduit and trunking work, motor winding and repair, solar panel installation, inverter and UPS wiring, electrical panel making, circuit design and troubleshooting, earthing and grounding systems, and safety switch installation.

From basic home electrical work to complex industrial installations, our curriculum prepares you to handle any electrical challenge professionally and safely.

Practical Workshop Training

Forget classroom-only learning. We emphasize practical workshop training where each student gets individual working stations, real electrical components, and professional tools. You'll practice wiring, circuit making, and troubleshooting on actual setups under expert supervision.

Professional Electrical Tools & Equipment

We provide training with industry-standard electrical tools including digital multimeters, clamp meters, insulation testers, wire strippers, crimping tools, conduit benders, voltage testers, soldering irons, and complete electrical toolkit sets for professional work.

Certified Electrical Trainers

Learn from licensed electricians with 10+ years of field experience. Our trainers not only teach electrical techniques but also share safety protocols, wiring regulations, energy efficiency tips, and business insights for starting your own electrical services business.

Safety Standards & Code Compliance

Safety is our priority. We provide comprehensive training on electrical safety standards, Indian Electricity Rules, IS codes, proper grounding techniques, circuit protection, and emergency procedures to ensure you work safely and comply with all regulations.

Business & Entrepreneurship Guidance

OZZO provides complete business startup support for aspiring electrical contractors. Our training includes guidance on licensing requirements, tool investment, material sourcing, client acquisition, quotation making, and project management for running a successful electrical services business.
