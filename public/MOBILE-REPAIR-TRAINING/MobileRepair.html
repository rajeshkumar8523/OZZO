Mobile Repair Training Services

At OZZO, we provide expert mobile repair training designed to transform beginners into skilled technicians. Our comprehensive courses cover both hardware and software troubleshooting for all major smartphone brands, using industry-standard tools and real-world repair scenarios.

Types of Mobile Repair We Teach

Our mobile repair training program covers a complete range of smartphone repair techniques for all major brands and models. Whether you're interested in basic repairs or advanced motherboard-level servicing, we provide comprehensive training.

We train students in screen replacement (LCD/OLED), battery replacement, charging port repairs, camera module replacement, speaker and microphone repairs, water damage treatment, motherboard troubleshooting, IC and chip-level repairs, software flashing and unlocking, network and signal issues, and Bluetooth/Wi-Fi repairs.

Hands-On Practical Training

Forget theoretical learning only. We emphasize 80% practical training where each student gets individual working stations, real faulty mobile sets, and actual repair tools. You'll practice on live devices under expert supervision until you gain confidence.

Industry-Standard Tools & Equipment

We provide training with professional repair tools including multimeters, DC power supplies, hot air rework stations, soldering machines, magnifying lamps, screwdriver sets, opening tools, and specialized software for mobile diagnostics and flashing.

Expert Trainers with Industry Experience

Learn from certified mobile repair professionals with 8+ years of field experience. Our trainers not only teach repair techniques but also share business insights, customer handling skills, and industry secrets for running a successful mobile repair business.

Software & Hardware Comprehensive Training

We cover both hardware and software aspects thoroughly. Hardware training includes component identification, circuit tracing, soldering/desoldering, while software training covers Android/iOS flashing, FRP unlocking, pattern removal, virus removal, and operating system troubleshooting.

Business Startup Support

OZZO doesn't just teach repair - we help you start your own business. Our training includes guidance on shop setup, tool procurement, spare parts sourcing, pricing strategies, marketing techniques, and customer management to ensure you're ready to launch your mobile repair venture.

Join OZZO Mobile Repair Training and master the skills needed for a successful career in the growing mobile repair industry. Whether you want to start your own business or join a service center, our training provides complete knowledge and practical expertise.
