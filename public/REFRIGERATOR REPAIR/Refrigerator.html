Refrigerator Repair Training Services

At OZZO, we provide comprehensive refrigerator repair training that transforms beginners into skilled technicians. Our hands-on courses cover all types of refrigerators, from domestic units to commercial systems, using professional tools and real-world repair scenarios.

Types of Refrigerator Repair We Teach

Our refrigerator repair training program covers complete servicing techniques for all types of cooling systems. Whether you're interested in basic maintenance or advanced refrigeration system repairs, we provide end-to-end training.

We train students in compressor diagnosis and replacement, gas charging (R134a, R600a), thermostat and temperature control repair, defrost system troubleshooting, condenser and evaporator coil cleaning, capillary tube blockage removal, ice maker repair, door seal replacement, PCB and electronic control board repair, and refrigerator electrical circuit troubleshooting.

From common issues like cooling problems and gas leakage to complex compressor and PCB repairs, our curriculum prepares you to handle any refrigerator repair challenge professionally.

Hands-On Practical Training with Live Units

Forget theoretical learning only. We emphasize practical training where each student works on actual faulty refrigerators. You'll practice gas charging, compressor replacement, and electrical repairs under expert supervision until you gain complete confidence.

Professional Refrigeration Tools & Equipment

We provide training with professional refrigeration tools including manifold gauges, vacuum pumps, gas charging cylinders, leak detectors, multimeters, tube cutters and benders, flaring tools, soldering kits, and specialized diagnostic equipment for refrigeration systems.

Expert Trainers with Field Experience

Learn from certified refrigeration technicians with 10+ years of field experience. Our trainers not only teach repair techniques but also share business insights, customer handling skills, and industry secrets for running a successful refrigerator repair service.

Domestic & Commercial System Training

We cover both domestic refrigerators and commercial cooling systems. Domestic training includes single-door, double-door, and side-by-side refrigerators. Commercial training covers deep freezers, ice cream cabinets, water coolers, and display refrigerators used in shops and restaurants.

Business Startup & Service Setup Support

OZZO doesn't just teach repair - we help you start your own business. Our training includes guidance on service center setup, tool procurement, spare parts sourcing, pricing strategies for different repairs, and marketing techniques to attract customers in your area.
