Types of Tailoring & Maggam Techniques We Teach

Our training program covers a complete range of tailoring and Maggam embroidery techniques for traditional and contemporary fashion. Whether you're interested in basic stitching or advanced Maggam artistry, we provide comprehensive skill development.

We train students in basic to advanced tailoring (blouses, dresses, suits, ethnic wear), Maggam embroidery (traditional South Indian designs), zardozi work, sequin and bead embroidery, appliqué work, patchwork, fabric manipulation techniques, pattern making and drafting, fitting and alterations, and machine and hand embroidery techniques.

From simple stitching to intricate Maggam patterns, our curriculum prepares you to create professional-grade garments and embroidery work for both personal and commercial purposes.

Hands-On Practical Training

We believe in learning by doing. Each student gets individual sewing machines, embroidery frames, and practice materials. You'll work on real garments and fabrics under expert guidance, progressing from simple stitches to complex Maggam patterns through structured practical sessions.

Traditional & Modern Equipment Training

We provide training with both traditional hand tools and modern equipment including sewing machines (manual, electric, computerized), Maggam frames and hooks, embroidery machines, cutting tools, measuring instruments, and specialized Maggam needles and threads for different fabric types.

Expert Artisans as Trainers

Learn from master tailors and Maggam artists with 10+ years of experience in traditional and contemporary fashion. Our trainers preserve authentic techniques while incorporating modern design sensibilities, ensuring you learn both heritage crafts and current fashion trends.

Design & Creative Development

Beyond technical skills, we nurture your creative abilities. Training includes fabric selection, color coordination, design adaptation, Maggam pattern creation, traditional motif preservation, and custom design development for weddings, festivals, and special occasions.

Business & Entrepreneurship Support

OZZO provides complete business guidance for aspiring tailoring entrepreneurs. We teach shop setup, client management, pricing strategies, marketing for custom tailoring services, bulk order handling, and how to establish a Maggam specialty boutique or home-based tailoring business.
