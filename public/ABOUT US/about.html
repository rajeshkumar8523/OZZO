About Us

OZZO is a fast-growing multi-service platform designed to make daily life easier, cleaner, and more convenient. From doorstep car washing and deep home cleaning to self-drive cars, fashion accessories, and grocery delivery — we bring premium quality services under one roof.

  • Expanding franchise network across cities

Whether you're a customer looking for dependable home services or an entrepreneur seeking franchise opportunities, OZZO is your go-to destination. We are committed to transforming local services into organized, reliable, and tech-powered experiences.

Join us in our mission to build a smarter, cleaner, and more connected world — one service at a time.
